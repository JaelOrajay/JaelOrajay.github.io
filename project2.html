Vexel Arts

In my graphic design coursework, I had the opportunity to delve into the captivating world of vexel art, a technique that combines vector and pixel elements to create stunningly detailed and vibrant compositions. For my final project in graphic design, I chose to feature Lee Jung Suk, a talented Korean artist, as the subject of my vexel artwork. I invested time and passion into capturing the essence of Lee Jung Suk's artistic persona. My vexel art piece featuring Lee Jung Suk earned recognition from my teacher, being selected as one of the best designs in the class. This acknowledgment not only validates my technical skills but also serves as a testament to the power of creativity and expression in the realm of graphic design.
