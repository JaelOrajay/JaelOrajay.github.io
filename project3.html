Airbnb QR Code-based Smart Door Lock System(Capstone)

The developed Airbnb QR Code-based Smart Door Lock System is a technology that unlocks the door through the use of generated QR code. It has an online service platform where guests may create, update and recover their accounts. The tenant can be notified when someone tries to access the door with an invalid QR code and can track the time of arrival of guests. Also, it can immediately identify intruders through face recognition.

Features for Tenants:
✓ Home
✓ About
✓ Services
✓ Register
✓ Login

Features for Lanlord:
✓ Houses
✓ Add House
✓ Activity Logs
✓ Invalid Key Attempts
