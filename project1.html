Operational Plan Record Management System(OplanRMS)

During my internship as a web developer, I undertook the exciting challenge of developing an Operational Plan Record Management System. This application is designed to add, manage, delete, and organize an organization's records and documents systematically and efficiently. It serves as a centralized repository for storing and managing electronic records. This dynamic system not only enables users to seamlessly sign up and log in but also empowers them to effortlessly add, manage, and monitor records, providing a comprehensive solution for effective data handling.

The key features of an OplanRMS are as follows:
- Create account and login
- Adding of records
- Manage records
- Search Functionality
