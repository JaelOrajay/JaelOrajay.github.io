LUOE(Laguna University Onlline Examination System)

The Laguna University Online Examination System(LUOE) holds profound significance as an innovative solution in education technology. Developed to address the evolving needs of academic assessment, this system serves as a digital bridge between administrators and examinees. Its purpose is twofold: Firstly, for administrators, it streamlines the management and addition of exams, introducing efficiency and organization into the assessment process. Secondly, for examinees, often students, it offers a user-friendly platform to take exams and promptly access results, fostering a seamless and accessible examination experience. It is created because of our web development final project, This project embodies the collective effort of my group, showcasing a dedication to advancing education through digital means.

Features for Admin:
✓ Course
✓ Add Exam
✓ Manage Exam
✓ Manage Examinee
✓ Ranking
✓ Feedback

Features for Examinee:
✓ Take Exam
✓ View Results
✓ Feedback
